# Live samples excluded from publication

The browser-sniff live captures (`user.json`, `requisitions.json`, `results.json`,
`results-report.json`) are intentionally not included in the published manuscripts:
they are raw API responses from a real member account and contain personal health
information. The schema knowledge they produced is captured in
`browser-sniff-summary.md` and `traffic-analysis.json`, which are PII-free.
